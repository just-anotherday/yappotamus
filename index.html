About Me

I'm a senior at the University of Central Florida studying Computer Science. Born and raised in New York City, I now call Oviedo, Florida home, where I've cultivated a strong work ethic and curiosity for problem-solving.

After high school at Francis Lewis High School, where I was part of the ROTC program, I worked at T-Mobile while starting studies in accounting at Baruch College. Early on, I realized I needed a more purposeful path and decided to redirect my focus toward a field I was truly passionate about.

I enlisted in the U.S. Army and was stationed at Fort Bragg, North Carolina, where I pushed myself to grow under pressure, mastering discipline, leadership, and resilience. After my service, I set my sights on Orlando, Florida to tackle new challenges in technology and education, determined to turn curiosity into real-world impact.

Today, I'm passionate about AI, backend development, and building software that solves real-world problems. Every step of my journey has shaped how I approach challenges with focus, curiosity, and the drive to build something meaningful.
