ASSEMBLY

STEP 1: Final Preparation

Heat the soup to a rolling boil and strain any of the impurities on top as shown in the photo.

STEP 2: Plating

In a bowl, place a portion of noodles, followed by topping and vegetables. Garnish with white pepper, sesame oil, and scallions to your preference.

STEP 3: Serving

Pour the hot soup over and garnish with crispy anchovies!

Serve immediately and top with more ingredients as everyone please!
