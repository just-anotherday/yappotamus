INGREDIENTS

DELICIOUS INGREDIENTS

1 bunch of Yu Choy, cleaned and prewashed
Vegetable oil, for frying
100 grams (g) dried anchovy, heads removed

ANCHOVY SOUP BASE

4 cups (1 quart) water
1 pounds (lbs.) pork bones
0.25 lb. dried anchovy
0.5 large yellow onion, halves
1 teaspoon (tsp) fish sauce, to taste
0.5 tsp white peppercorn, crushed
0.5 thumb-size ginger, quartered
1 honey date

NOODLES/DOUGH

2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon (tbsp) vegetable oil
0.75 cup water + 0.25 cup water
0.5 tsp salt
0.5 tsp sugar
0.5 tbsp cornstarch

GROUND PORK AND SHIITAKE TOPPING

0.5 lb. ground pork
6 pieces dried shiitake mushroom soaked + stems removed, keep mushroom juices!
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 shallot, minced
0.5 thumb-size ginger, minced
0.5 tsp chicken bouillon powder
1 tbsp fish sauce

DIPPING SAUCE (SPICY)

6 Thai chili-peppers, cut to small pieces
soy sauce, to taste
